the unoccupied orbital in
Fe3+
to form the coordination bond according

the adjacent perfluorinated carbon chain including the attached F atoms

decrease the fluorine nuclear magnetism. The chemical shift at

apparently decreased. It well corresponded with the significant decrease
(by 55.6%) in atomic point charge of F23 & F24 using DFT calculations
(Table S2). This phenomenon further decreases the electron density of
the perfluorinated carbon chain and suppresses the electron shielding

attack.
