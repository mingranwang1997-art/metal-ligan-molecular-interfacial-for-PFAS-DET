ABSTRACT: The development of alternative energy systems for the clean
production, storage, and conversion of energy is strongly dependent on our

stability relationships are still missing. This is primarily because there is no
adequate experimental approach capable of monitoring the stability of well-
defined single crystals in situ. Here, by utilizing the power of inductively
coupled plasma mass spectrometry (ICP-MS) connected to a stationary
probe and coupling this technique to the rotating disk electrode method, it
